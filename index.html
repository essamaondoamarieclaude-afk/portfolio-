The Gap

Most teams build fast or build secure — rarely both. And design? Usually an afterthought. These are the structural problems I was built to solve.

[01]

Products ship with no visual identity

Developers build the logic, design gets bolted on last minute. The result feels amateur even when the code is solid.

[02]

Security is added after the breach

Teams wait until something breaks to think about pentesting and threat modeling. By then, the damage is done.

[03]

Full-stack means frontend OR backend

Finding someone who can own the whole stack and also care about the interface is rare. I'm that person.

[04]

Automation built without rigor breaks

Trading bots and automated systems built without precision cost more to maintain than to build. I build them right.

The fix isn't hiring three people. It's working with one engineer who thinks about all three layers from the start.

Who I Am

I'm a Computer Science Engineer based in Douala, Cameroon — passionate about the intersection of technology and business. I build full-stack products, craft interfaces people actually enjoy using, and think hard about how systems can be made more secure.
